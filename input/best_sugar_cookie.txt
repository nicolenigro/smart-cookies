360 grams all-purpose flour, measured then sifted
6 grams baking powder
4.9 grams fine sea salt
339 grams unsalted butter, softened
297 grams granulated sugar + extra for rolling
100 grams eggs
14 grams vanilla

Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. 
2. Mix the flour, baking powder, salt, and any other dry ingredients (not sugar) in a medium bowl.
2. Cream the butter and sugar together on high until light and fluffy. Then turn the mixer on low and add the eggs and vanilla extract, and any remaining wet ingredients.
3. With the mixer running on low, slowly add the flour mixture and any mix-ins.
4. Pour some extra sugar into a bowl to coat the cookies. Scoop the dough out and roll into 1-inch balls.
5. Bake each sheet of sugar cookies for 9-11 minutes.
