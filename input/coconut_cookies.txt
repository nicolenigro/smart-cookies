150 grams all purpose flour
3 grams baking soda
0.66 grams salt
56.5 grams butter
106.5 grams brown sugar 
99 grams sugar
50 grams egg
2.33 grams vanilla 
80 grams coconut 

Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F/175 degrees C.
2. In a small mixing bowl add the the flour, baking soda, salt, and any additional dry ingredients. Mix and set aside.
3. In a separate medium sized bowl add the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar. Cream them together until smooth.
4. Add the egg and vanilla. Beat until light and fluffy. Beat in any additional wet ingredients.
5. Add the flour, a little at a time, until incorporated. Mix in the mix-ins.
6. Drop, 3 inches apart, onto ungreased cookie sheet.
7. Bake until lightly toasted, about 8-12 minutes. Move to a wire rack and cool.
