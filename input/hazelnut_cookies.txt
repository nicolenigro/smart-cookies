170.1 grams hazelnut
27.2 grams vegetable oil
84.8 grams unsalted butter, at room temperature
106.5 grams dark brown sugar
66 grams granulated sugar
50 grams egg
2.3 grams vanilla
120 grams flour
3 grams baking soda

Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Place the mix-ins in a food processor and grind coarsely. Grind the remaining mix-ins fine and add the oil. Blend the mixture thoroughly into a butter. Set aside.
3. Cream the butter and sugars together until light and fluffy. Beat in the mix-in butter, the egg, the vanilla, and any remaining wet ingredients. Mix thoroughly.
4. Sift the flour and add with the baking soda, salt, and remaining dry ingredients. Using a teaspoon, form the dough into balls and arrange them 2 inches apart on baking sheets that have been greased or lined with parchment paper. Using the prongs of a fork that have been dipped into flour to prevent sticking, flatten the balls into 1 1/2 inch rounds 
5. Bake in the middle of the oven for 8-to-9 minutes.
