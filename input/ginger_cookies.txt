186.5 grams butter
198 grams sugar
50 grams egg
85 grams molasses
270 grams all-purpose flour
8 grams ginger
4 grams baking soda
3 grams cinnamon
2 grams clove
1 grams salt

Instructions
1. In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in egg and any other wet ingredients
2. Combine the flour, baking soda, spices, salt, and remaining dry ingredients; gradually add to the creamed mixture and mix well. Gently add in any mix-ins.
3. Roll into 1-1/2-in. balls, then roll in sugar. Place 2 in. apart on ungreased baking sheets. 
4. Bake at 350Â° until puffy and lightly browned, 10-12 minutes. Remove to wire racks to cool.
