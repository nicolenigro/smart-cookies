360 grams all-purpose flour
12 grams baking powder
18 grams baking soda
7.8 grams cinnamon
6 grams salt
339 grams butter, at room temperature
297 grams granulated sugar
319.5 grams light-brown sugar
150 grams eggs
14 grams vanilla
510 grams chocolate
267 grams oats
120 grams coconut
228 grams pecan

Instructions
1. Heat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and any other dry ingredients in bowl.
3. In a very large bowl, beat butter with an electric mixer at medium speed until smooth and creamy. Gradually beat in sugars, and combine thoroughly.
4. Add eggs one at a time, beating after each. Beat in vanilla. Beat in additional wet ingredients.
5. Stir in flour mixture until just combined. Stir in mix-ins.
6. For each cookie, drop 1/4 cup dough onto ungreased baking sheets, spacing 3 inches apart.
7. Bake for 15 to 17 minutes, until edges are lightly browned; rotate sheets halfway through. Remove cookies from rack to cool.
