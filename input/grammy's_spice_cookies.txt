169.5 grams unsalted butter, softened
200 grams granulated sugar
85 grams molasses
50 grams egg
240 grams all-purpose flour
12 grams baking soda
2.6 grams cinnamon
1.3 grams ginger
1.63 grams clove
3 grams fine sea salt

Instructions
1. Heat oven to 375 degrees. Line several baking sheets with parchment paper or nonstick liners.
2. Using an electric mixer, beat tablespoons butter with the granulated sugar, egg, and any additional wet ingredients until fluffy, about 2 minutes. Slowly beat in flour, baking soda, spices,  salt, and all other dry ingredients. Gently mix in any mix-ins.
3. Shape dough into walnut-size balls and place 2 inches apart on baking sheets. Bake until firm, about 10 to 12 minutes. Let cool on wire racks.
