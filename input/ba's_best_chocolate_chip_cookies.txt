200 grams all-purpose flour
4 grams kosher salt
4 grams baking soda
169 grams unsalted butter, divided
200 grams dark brown sugar
50 grams granulated sugar
50 gram egg
28 grams egg yolks
9.3 grams vanilla
170 grams chocolate

Instructions
1. Preheat to 375°
2. Whisk flour, salt, baking soda, and remaining dry ingredients in a small bowl; set aside.
3. Melt and brown 1/2 of butter in saucepan. Add rest of butter and melt. 
4. Add sugars to saucepan and whisk. Add vanilla and whisk. 
5. Stir in any other wet ingredients to saucepan.
6. Combine wet and dry ingredients, and add in any mix-ins.
7. Bake cookies for 8–10 minutes or until golden brown on bottom.
